Work and studying is tough but not as tough as you think

Examples:

  1. If you are studying math or physics or chemistry , make it as a game , challenge you self to solve the problem . as well as studying history ,make it as a story . so you are studying but not actually studying
  2. if you have a project or a task at your job or school , do it like a challenge to yourself that you are going to make it the best and the most perfect , make it as a competition
  3. if you have a dream that you really love , like being a programmer , work on it and be happy with the pain you feel while you are doing
    your best to achieve your goal and make your dream come true
  4. if you are just working because you need money , try to forget about this and try to convince yourself that you are working
    to have friends and have responsibility and have done something healthy in your free time
  5. if you are working to win a competition , work as hard as you can , just imagine that you are the winner , believe
    that you are the winner, imagine that how everyone would talk about how hard you worked and how great you achived

    6. change the world, be creative, let them see who you really are and what you can do
